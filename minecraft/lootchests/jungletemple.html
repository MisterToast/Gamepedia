Jungle Temple

There are two Chests in a Jungle Temple. The Jungle Temple can only generate in a normal Jungle Biom and in a Bamboo Jungle.

Chest 1:

The first Chest is locatet behind the Riddle. When you entre the Jungle Temple you first have to go downstairs. Than you turn left and break the Block behind the middle Lever.

Chest 2:

The second Chest is locatet under the entrence. When you entre the Jungle Temple you first have to go downstairs. Than you turn right and follow the tunnel. Be sure to not be hitted by the arrows, you can easily break the strings on the floor to not trigger the trap. At the end should be the Chest.
